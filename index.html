The Legend

Kevin Parissien, the charismatic frontman of The Honeymoon Suites, emerged from Cape Town's vibrant music scene in the 1990s as one of South Africa's most captivating glamrock performers.

With his magnetic stage presence and distinctive vocal style, Kevin led The Honeymoon Suites to become one of Cape Town's most beloved bands. Known for their theatrical performances, outrageous costumes, and infectious energy, the band captured the hearts of fans across South Africa.
