Fan Memories

Oudtshoorn Casino Experience

"By the end of the week they themselves were dressing up as well and dancing on the tables. They had this old SA flag and a new SA flag next to each other on the wall."

- From the Klein Karoo Festival residency
The Beehive Hair Fans

"Like those fans in Oudtshoorn who had their hair piled up on their heads in those beehive hairstyles, they looked like the Marge Simpson sisters."

- Bood Carver remembering the dedicated fans
The Tongue Incident

"After one of our gigs in Oudtshoorn this woman of about 35 came up to me and asked if she could stick her tongue in my mouth... She sang me this song about a fallen woman that was her own composition."

- Kevin Parissien's memorable fan encounter
