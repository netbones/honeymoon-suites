Album Notes

About This Collection

This greatest hits compilation brings together the most beloved tracks from The Honeymoon Suites' legendary career in the South African music scene. From the breakthrough hit "Luminous Anonymity" to the epic "Quality Babe," this collection showcases the band's unique blend of glamrock theatrics and lounge-lizard sophistication.

Featuring remastered versions of classics from the "Five Star" album, plus rare B-sides and previously unreleased tracks like "Cape Town Nights" and "Graceland Dreams," this compilation offers both longtime fans and newcomers the definitive Honeymoon Suites experience.

Kevin Parissien's distinctive vocals, combined with the band's theatrical stage presence and innovative arrangements, created a sound that was uniquely South African yet universally appealing. This collection preserves that magic for future generations.
