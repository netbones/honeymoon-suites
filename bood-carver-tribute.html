His Lasting Legacy

Bood Carver's influence on The Honeymoon Suites and South African music continues to resonate, inspiring new generations of musicians and fans.

Multi-Instrumentalist

Master of bass and guitar, Bood brought versatility and depth to The Honeymoon Suites' sound. His ability to switch between instruments during performances added dynamic energy to their live shows.

Songwriter

Starting at age seven, Bood's songwriting journey spanned decades. His compositions for The Honeymoon Suites reflected his diverse influences, from 70s pop to contemporary alternative rock.

Set Designer

Beyond music, Bood was a set builder and designer, creating the elaborate backdrops and stage designs that made Honeymoon Suites performances unforgettable visual experiences.

Band Brother

A Scorpio with passion and intensity, Bood was more than a bandmate - he was family. His contributions to the band's creative process and his friendship with Kevin and Julia were irreplaceable.

South African Music Pioneer

Part of the 90s Cape Town music scene that challenged conventions and brought glamrock to South Africa. His work helped define a generation of alternative music in the country.

Eternal Influence

HMS Bood stands as a testament to his enduring impact. Released in 2017, it ensures that his musical voice continues to reach new audiences and inspire future musicians.
